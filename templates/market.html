Indian Market Relevance

India is witnessing rapid growth in retail participation in financial markets, rising income volatility, and increasing exposure to long-term financial products. However, most individuals still rely on static tools or informal advice.

FinTwin addresses this gap by enabling users to simulate financial decisions in advance and understand long-term consequences using a personalized Digital Financial Twin.

Key Problems in the Indian Context

  • Low financial literacy despite high smartphone penetration
  • Under-preparedness for retirement and long-term goals
  • Increasing debt-driven lifestyle spending
  • Limited access to personalized financial planning

How FinTwin Solves This

  • Moves financial planning from static advice to interactive simulation
  • Encourages informed decision-making before major life events
  • Provides explainable insights instead of complex jargon
  • Supports responsible financial behavior through foresight
