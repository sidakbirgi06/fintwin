About FinTwin AI

A WealthTech platform built to improve financial decision-making.

What is a Digital Financial Twin?

A Digital Financial Twin is a virtual representation of an individualâ€™s financial life. It mirrors income, expenses, assets, liabilities, and goals to create a realistic financial model.

FinTwin uses this model to simulate decisions and analyze outcomes before they happen in real life.

How FinTwin Works

  1. Financial Data Collection: Users enter basic financial information such as income, expenses, assets, liabilities, and goals.
  2. Twin Creation: The platform creates a Digital Financial Twin based on these inputs.
  3. Scenario Simulation: Users test decisions like increasing investments or buying a house on a cloned version of the twin.
  4. Impact Analysis: FinTwin compares outcomes and generates explainable insights.

FinTech Pillars Used

WealthTech: For long-term financial planning, goal analysis, and investment-related decision support.

RegTech (Supporting Layer): To ensure transparency, explainability, and responsible use of financial data.
