What is FinTwin?

FinTwin AI is a WealthTech platform that creates a Digital Financial Twin of an individual. It allows users to simulate financial decisions, test life choices, and understand long-term impact before acting in the real world.

What Problem Does It Solve?

Most people make financial decisions using static calculators or generic advice. These tools fail to account for behavior, life events, and long-term consequences.

FinTwin solves this by offering personalized, forward-looking financial simulations instead of reactive tracking.
